Coding To Complete The Blockchain

Deep Dive Code-A-Thon is a hackathon style competition. Competitors will form teams and build an app in 40 hours. The winning teams will walk away with prizes, including a first place prize $500 per person, a second place prize of $250 per person, and a third place prize of $125 sponsored by Deep Dive Coding.

How

  • Teams formed, mix of seniors, mids, and junior developers
  • Select a tech stack
  • 40 hours to create new app
  • Projects will be judged by panel of senior developers
  • For a full list of rules and technical documentation visit the repo.

Money

  • $25 - Early bird tickets before September 15th
  • $45 - Regular price, at-the-door pricing.

All proceeds go to the Deep Dive Alumni Associaction for future events.
